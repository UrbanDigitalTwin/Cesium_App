    Box Filters

    Bounding Box

    Draw and manage analysis areas on the map.

    Controls Guide

    Create

    Click Create, then click and drag on the map to draw a box. Right-click to cancel.

    Edit

    Click Edit to show handles. Drag the corners to resize the box. Click Save to confirm or Cancel to revert.

    Activate / Deactivate

    Select filters, then click Activate to run analysis. The box will turn cyan. Click Deactivate to stop.

    Delete

    Click Delete to permanently remove the bounding box from the map.
